# Visual Concept Types — Animated Math Visuals

Each visual concept is a 3-second stick-figure animation template that shows a math concept
with actual numbers. The same template is reused across grades by changing the numbers.
One short caption sentence accompanies each animation.

## Summary: 52 visual concept types covering 147 clusters (509 standards)

---

## 1. COUNTING & NUMBER SENSE (K-2)

**VC-01: Counting objects** — Stick figure points at objects one by one, numbers appear above each.
- K.CC.A (count to 100), K.CC.B (count objects), 1.NBT.A (count to 120)
- Caption: "Count each one — the last number tells you how many."

**VC-02: Comparing quantities** — Two groups of objects side by side, stick figure points to the bigger group.
- K.CC.C (compare numbers), K.MD.A (compare measurable attributes), K.MD.B (classify & count)
- Caption: "Which group has more?"

**VC-03: Composing teens** — Ten-frame fills up, extra ones appear beside it.
- K.NBT.A (compose 11-19 as ten + ones)
- Caption: "Ten ones make a ten. Then count the extras."

## 2. ADDITION & SUBTRACTION (K-3)

**VC-04: Putting together** — Stick figure pushes two groups together, total appears.
- K.OA.A (add/subtract with objects), 1.OA.A (word problems within 20), 2.OA.A (within 100)
- Caption: "Put them together to find how many in all."

**VC-05: Taking apart** — Group of objects, stick figure removes some, remaining count appears.
- 1.OA.B (properties of operations), 2.OA.B (fluent add/subtract within 20)
- Caption: "Take some away — how many are left?"

**VC-06: Equal sign balance** — Simple equation with a balance visual: left side = right side.
- 1.OA.D (meaning of equal sign), 1.OA.C (relate counting to add/subtract)
- Caption: "Both sides must be the same."

**VC-07: Odd and even** — Objects arranged in pairs, last one alone or paired.
- 2.OA.C (odd/even, arrays)
- Caption: "Pair them up — is there one left over?"

## 3. PLACE VALUE & BASE TEN (1-5)

**VC-08: Place value blocks** — Hundreds/tens/ones blocks stack and regroup.
- 1.NBT.B (place value, two digits), 2.NBT.A (three digits), 3.NBT.A (rounding)
- Caption: "Each place is worth 10 times more."

**VC-09: Multi-digit addition** — Numbers stack vertically, carry animation.
- 1.NBT.C (add within 100), 2.NBT.B (add/subtract within 1000), 4.NBT.B (multi-digit operations)
- Caption: "Line up the places. Carry when you get 10 or more."

**VC-10: Powers of 10** — Number shifts left/right as ×10 or ÷10, decimal point moves.
- 4.NBT.A (place value to millions), 5.NBT.A (powers of 10, decimals), 5.NBT.B (decimal operations)
- Caption: "Multiply by 10 — every digit shifts one place left."

## 4. MULTIPLICATION & DIVISION (3-5)

**VC-11: Equal groups** — Stick figure arranges objects into equal rows.
- 3.OA.A (interpret products), 3.OA.B (properties of multiplication)
- Caption: "3 groups of 4 = 12. That's multiplication."

**VC-12: Multiplication fluency** — Flash: 7 × 8 → array fills → 56 appears.
- 3.OA.C (fluently multiply/divide within 100)
- Caption: "Know your facts — they're the building blocks."

**VC-13: Multi-step word problems** — Stick figure reads problem, two operations shown in sequence.
- 3.OA.D (two-step problems), 4.OA.A (multiplicative comparison), 5.OA.A (order of operations)
- Caption: "Break it into steps. Solve one at a time."

**VC-14: Factors and patterns** — Factor pairs fan out from a number; pattern arrows.
- 4.OA.B (factors/multiples), 4.OA.C (patterns), 5.OA.B (two numerical patterns)
- Caption: "Every number has factor pairs hiding inside."

## 5. FRACTIONS (3-5)

**VC-15: What is a fraction** — Bar splits into equal parts, one part shaded.
- 3.NF.A (understand fractions, number line, equivalence)

**VC-16: Equivalent fractions** — Two fraction bars side by side, same amount shaded.
- 4.NF.A (fraction equivalence and ordering)
- Caption: "Different pieces, same amount."

**VC-17: Add/subtract fractions** — Fraction bars align to same denominator, then combine.
- 4.NF.B (add/subtract fractions with like denominators), 5.NF.A (unlike denominators)

**VC-18: Multiply/divide fractions** — Area model shading: row × column = overlap.
- 4.NF.C (decimal fractions), 5.NF.B (multiply/divide fractions)
- Caption: "Multiply fractions: shade the overlap."

**VC-29: Coordinate plane** — Axes appear, point plots at (x,y), label shows.

**VC-30: Lines and angles** — Points, lines, rays drawn; angle types labeled.

**VC-31: Area of complex shapes** — Triangle/trapezoid decomposed into rectangles.

**VC-32: Scale drawings** — Shape enlarges/shrinks proportionally with scale factor.

**VC-33: Circle formulas** — Radius spins to draw circle, pi × d = circumference.

**VC-36: Volume formulas** — Cone/cylinder/sphere with formula overlay.
