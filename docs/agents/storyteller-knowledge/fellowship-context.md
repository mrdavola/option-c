# Fellowship Context for The Storyteller

Source: Barbara's Notion workspace, April 2026. Worldwide Venture Fellowship.

- **Program:** Worldwide Venture Fellowship for AI in Education (Spring 2026)
- **Duration:** 100 days (12 weeks)

- **Structure:** Teams of 2-4 with roles: Builders, Hustlers, Storytellers

## Success metrics (the floor, not the ceiling)

1. **50+ user feedback sessions** (real conversations, not surveys)
2. **First paying customer** (even $1 counts)
3. **Ship production-ready prototype** with AI-native features
4. **10k+ views** on a build-in-public post (go viral 1x)
5. **Weekly channel** to engage customers directly

## Barbara's advantage

- Founder of **Acton Academy Falls Church** (learner-driven school, DC area)
- Part of a network of **300+ Acton Academies** — potential pilot sites
- Already ran a challenge: **11 learners (ages 10-16)** designed video games teaching math concepts
- Has direct access to learners for daily testing

## Problem statement (Barbara's original pitch)

"In traditional education, academic concepts are often taught in isolation, making it hard for learners to see why they matter. When learning feels abstract or disconnected from real life, engagement drops."

**Proposal:** AI tool that generates project-based learning quests beginning with learner interests, embedding required academic skills.

**Feedback received:** Make it data-driven and specific. Show career pathways with simulation-based learning.

## Content strategy requirements

- **Build in public:** 2x social posts per week individually + 1 team blog post
- **Channels:** LinkedIn (professional), Twitter/X (tech community), fellowship Slack
- **Tone:** Founder perspective, honest about failures, data-driven
- **Memo format for presentations:** Intro user → Problem → Status quo → Solution → Demo → Pricing → Team → Next steps → Ask
